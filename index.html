Years taken to repay the loan

This number is the number of years that it takes after graduation to repay the loan. If this number is 50, then there is still debt remaining 50 years after graduation.

Remaining debt

This model assumes that the student retires 50 years after graduating from university. This number reflects the amount of debt remaining on the loan in today's dollars when the student retires. It is shown in red in the graph.

Interest paid

This number represents the total amount of interest paid for the education in today's dollars. This number is shown in blue in the graph.
